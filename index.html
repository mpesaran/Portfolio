Who is Mahsa?

Developer based in Melbourne

I’m a Software Developer with a passion for solving problems and creating meaningful solutions. My journey started in the world of entrepreneurship, where I built my own business and discovered how much I enjoy combining creativity with technology to tackle challenges head-on. That’s what led me to pursue a career in software development.

My technical foundation began at Victoria University Polytechnic, where I studied software engineering and gained a strong grasp of development principles. From there, I expanded my skills through online platforms like Udemy, Coursera, and FreeCodeCamp—because in this industry, learning never stops. Now, I’m taking things to the next level by studying Software Engineering at Holberton School, where I’m diving deeper into the craft and preparing for bigger challenges ahead.

Outside of coding, I stay inspired by exploring the world—traveling to new destinations, trying different cuisines, and learning from diverse cultures. Closer to home, I enjoy experimenting in the kitchen and tending to my vegetable garden. Both remind me that growth, whether in life or technology, comes from patience, creativity, and a willingness to try something new.
