Todo List Mobile App:Easy Planner

An application to organize daily tasks

Easy Planner is an Android app that offers a number of features and is designed with the user in mind. Through this intuitive app, users can take control of their daily tasks, streamline their activities, and maximize their productivity.

Key Features:

Effortless Task Management: The app offers a seamless and efficient way to manage tasks. Users can effortlessly create, edit, and delete tasks, enabling them to maintain an organized schedule with ease.

Persistent Task Storage: With the integration of AsyncStorage, the Todo List App ensures that users' tasks are securely stored and synchronized across devices. This means that even if you close the app or switch devices, your tasks will always be accessible.

Streamlined Task Tracking: Users can efficiently manage both short and long task lists. The app employs a scrollable list, allowing for easy navigation through tasks. Completed tasks can be swiftly removed with a single tap.
